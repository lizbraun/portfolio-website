DMD Hub

A website made by senior Digital Media and Design students for our 2022 practicum class. The site uses HTML, CSS, and Javascript and was created by a group of 6 students over the course of the semester. This was made to help DMD students in course selection and overall awareness within the DMD program. The site includes offered courses, DMD events, internship/job search, an offered equipment page, and a resource page with forms such as applying for graduation and information on plans of study. We have designed this site in multiple sprints and worked in an agile environment.

DMD Hub

I worked in a team to design 30 motion graphics and mockups of 2021-2022 UConn basketball players to be displayed on two-story tall LED boards in the Werth Family UConn Basketball Champions Center. We communicated weekly with UConn clients on zoom or in person, presenting mockups and receiving any feedback. I worked with a student design team where I embraced teamwork and gained excellent communication skills with my team members and clients. This experience was in collaboration with UConn senior DMD class Agency.
