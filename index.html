DMD Hub

Web Design | UI/UX Design

DMD Hub home page

DMD Hub made by senior Digital Media and Design students for the UConn Web Design Practicum class. The site uses HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Javascript and was created by a group of 6 students over the course of the semester. This was made to help DMD students in course selection and overall awareness within the DMD program. The site includes offered courses, DMD events, an internship/job search, an available equipment page, and a resource page with forms such as applying for graduation and information on plans of study. We designed this site in multiple sprints, worked in an agile environment, and user tested on DMD students and faculty.

UConn Athletics Champions Hall

I worked in a team to design 30 motion graphics and mockups of 2021-2022 UConn basketball players to be displayed on two-story tall LED boards in the Werth Family UConn Basketball Champions Center. We communicated weekly with UConn clients on zoom or in person, presenting mockups and receiving any feedback. I worked with a student design team where I embraced teamwork and gained excellent communication skills with my team members and clients. Each video has the same layout and format, but the information and photos are adapted for each player. This experience was in collaboration with UConn senior DMD class Agency.

CTDA Social Justics Collection

A currated collection of social justice content within the Connecticut Digital Archive online repository. For this project I designed the logo, edited cover photos and designed the color palette. I also collaborated in a team of 3 people to make the collection, design, and CTDA branding cohesive.
